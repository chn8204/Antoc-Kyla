About Me

Hi, I’m Donita Kyla S. Antoc, a BSIT student at Eastern Visayas State University - Carigara Campus. I believe life is better when you spend it doing what you love. Whether it’s designing, writing, coding, creating art, or learning new skills, I’m always looking for ways to turn passion into purpose.

I enjoy projects that challenge me, inspire creativity, and allow me to grow. For me, it’s not just about the final result—it’s about loving the process, too.

Outside the campus, I make time for the simple things that bring me joy: singing, reading books, cooking food, and bonding with my family.

This space is a reflection of what I love. Thanks for being here.
